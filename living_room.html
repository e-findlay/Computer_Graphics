Controls:

Press T to turn textures On/Off
Press P to play the Piano (Press P to stop)
Press L to swing the dangling light (Press L to stop)
Press M to move the TV cabinet, sofas and armchairs (Press M to stop)
Press X to move the piano stool (Press X to stop)
Press K to push a chair into the table (Press K again to reset)
Press C to open the TV cabinet doors (Press C to stop)
Use the arrow keys to change the viewing angle
Use the WS keys to change the z coordinates
Use the AD keys to change the xcoordinates
